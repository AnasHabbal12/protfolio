About Me

I have been interested in web development since I was a teenager. I started by learning the basics of HTML and CSS and creating simple websites for fun. Later, I enrolled in a web development course at the University of Damascus, where I learned more advanced topics such as JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and WordPress. I also participated in several online courses and projects to improve my skills and portfolio.

After graduating, I worked as a freelance web developer for various clients and companies, both locally and internationally. I developed websites for different purposes and industries, such as e-commerce, education, entertainment, and social media. I also collaborated with other developers and designers to create complex and dynamic web applications.

My goal is to become a senior web developer and lead a team of talented and passionate developers. I also want to contribute to the web development community by sharing my knowledge and experience and helping others learn and grow.
